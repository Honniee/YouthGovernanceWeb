Template Features & Updates

Design Features

  • Professional Design: Dark header with gradient (#2c3e50 to #34495e)
  • Circular Logo Container: White circle with logo image or "LYDO" text
  • Status Badges: Color-coded badges (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
  • Responsive Layout: Works on desktop and mobile devices
  • Consistent Branding: Municipality of San Jose, Batangas footer
  • Contact Information: Email and phone in footer

Privacy & Safety Features

  • Privacy Section: Comprehensive privacy & data protection information
  • Email Usage: Clear explanation of how email is used
  • Data Security: Information about secure storage and access
  • Confidentiality: Assurance of data confidentiality
  • User Rights: Explanation of user rights and how to contact
  • Enhanced Footer: Detailed privacy notice with contact information
  • No Account Required: Clarification that no account is needed

üìß About These Templates

These email templates are designed to reassure youth who submit surveys without creating accounts. Since youth only provide their email addresses, these templates include comprehensive privacy and safety messaging to ensure they feel secure using the system. The privacy sections explain how their data is protected, how their email is used, and what their rights are.

Note: The logo will appear if EMAIL_LOGO_URL or MUNICIPALITY_LOGO_URL environment variable is set. Contact information (email and phone) can be customized via CONTACT_EMAIL and CONTACT_PHONE environment variables.
