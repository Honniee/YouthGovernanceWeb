## 4. Personal Data We Collect

### 4.1 Youth Profile Information
- Full name (first, middle, last name, suffix)
- Date of birth and age
- Gender
- Contact information (email, phone number)
- Address (barangay, purok/zone)
- Educational background
- Employment status
- Voter registration status

### 4.2 Survey Responses
- Responses to KK Survey questionnaires
- Program interests and preferences
- Civic engagement activities
- Feedback and suggestions

### 4.3 System Usage Data
- Login credentials (email, password - encrypted)
- Activity logs
- IP address and device information
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

## 5. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Program Planning and Development**
   - Analyze youth demographics and needs
   - Design and improve youth programs and services
   - Generate statistical reports for policy development

2. **Survey Administration**
   - Conduct KK Survey and other youth assessments
   - Validate survey responses
   - Generate insights for program planning

3. **Service Delivery**
   - Manage youth profiles and accounts
   - Facilitate communication with youth
   - Process program registrations and applications

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Respond to lawful requests from government authorities
   - Protect legal rights and interests

5. **System Administration**
   - Maintain system security and functionality
   - Monitor system usage and performance
   - Conduct audits and investigations

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We may share your personal data only in the following circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent** - When you have explicitly consented to the sharing
2. **Service Providers** - With trusted service providers who assist in system operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
3. **Legal Requirements** - When required by law or legal process
4. **Government Authorities** - When necessary for compliance with government regulations
5. **Public Safety** - When necessary to protect public safety or prevent harm

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

- **Encryption** - Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Controls** - Role-based access control (RBAC) restricts data access to authorized personnel only
- **Regular Backups** - Automated daily backups ensure data availability and recovery
- **Security Monitoring** - Continuous monitoring for security incidents and breaches
- **Staff Training** - Regular training on data privacy and security

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

- **Youth Profiles** - 5 years after last activity, then anonymized
- **Survey Responses** - 5 years after creation, then anonymized
- **Activity Logs** - 90 days, then deleted
- **Audit Logs** - 1 year, then archived

After the retention period, personal data is either:
- **Anonymized** - Personal identifiers removed, aggregated data retained for statistical purposes
- **Deleted** - Permanently removed from our systems

## 9. Your Rights Under RA 10173

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

1. **Right to be Informed** - You have the right to know how your data is collected and used
2. **Right to Access** - You can request access to your personal data
3. **Right to Rectification** - You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
4. **Right to Erasure** - You can request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
5. **Right to Object** - You can object to processing of your personal data
6. **Right to Data Portability** - You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
7. **Right to File a Complaint** - You can file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)
